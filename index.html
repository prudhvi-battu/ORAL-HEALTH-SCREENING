Why Oral Health Matters?

Proper oral health care is essential for overall wellness, preventing issues like cavities, gum disease, and serious conditions such as oral cancer. Brushing twice daily, flossing, and using mouthwash help remove plaque and bacteria, keeping teeth and gums healthy. Regular dental checkups aid in early problem detection, preventing costly treatments. Good oral hygiene is also linked to overall health, reducing risks of heart disease and diabetes.
